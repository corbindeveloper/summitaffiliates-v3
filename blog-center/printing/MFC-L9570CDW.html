    We Are a Proud Authorized Brother Dealer

    The Award Winning MFC-L9570CDW Printer

    Are you a business looking for an excellent all-in-one printer that can meet your demanding printer volume. The award-winning Brother MFC-L9570CDW Printer may be the answer your business is looking for. With its impressive speeds reaching up to 33 pages per minute in both color and black, this machine, is a popular choice for many mid-sized workgroups, this multifunction printer will elevate your business by offering advanced security and productivity enhancing features.

    In this article, we will explore some of these features and benefits for what makes this printer such a great option.


    The video was made and presented by Brother South Africa


    Intuitive Color Touchscreen Display

    This printer features a 7” color Touchscreen display that offers easy navigation and creation of up to 64 customized shortcuts to reduce the complexity of frequently used tasks. Icons are also able be added or removed based on user preference. Tabs can be named and customized to create a screen that is familiar and beneficial to your business’ preferences.

    The touchscreen display also allows easy scanning or printing from various popular cloud services. These include examples such as Google Drive, Dropbox, Evernote, and much more.

    Class Leading Scalable Paper Handling

    This Brother machine comes with a 250-sheet capacity paper tray that can be adjusted to accommodate for letter or legal-size paper. Additionally, the printer also comes with a 50-sheet capacity multi-purpose tray for printing letterhead and other types of media to meet the printing needs of any business.

    Additionally optional paper trays for more capacity can be made available to be compatible with the printer. These are available in 250 sheet or 500 sheet capacities, allowing for 1,300 sheets total capacity.

    Alternatively, this machine is also capable of a tower try option that can include four 520-sheet trays that will transform the printer into a floor standing model. It is particularly useful for storing different media groups, increasing the total capacity to 2,380 sheets.

    High End Security Features

    Within the machine is an NFC card reader for easy badge authentication and Active Directory support for network user authentication. These features assist in protecting unauthorized users from gaining access to the printer and additionally offer better management of costs. The company will be able to restrict who can have access or what quantity a group may be able to print off the MFP.

    The secure function lock allows for administrators to regulate usage and certain functions on the printer further. Such as only allowing printing of color for these select groups.

    The secure print feature will allow a PIN to be required when accessing your printed content. Ensuring the person that printed the document is the person that will be receiving it.

    Additionally, the MFC-L9570CDW has support for network security protocols such as enterprise security, IPSec, SSL/TLS, and much more.

    Flexible Connectivity Options

    The printer can be shared with the whole work office via a wireless network. This provides incredible ease and efficiency. Employees will be able to print from their mobile devices from any location in the network. In addition to that, users can also utilize a USB that can connect into the printer and initiate prints of documents.

    Because of the security to withhold the prints before the user authorizes, this also makes printing from any location more relieving. The user won’t have to worry about being at the printer, because the job won’t be complete till the person initiating the printer is there. This also provides flexibility in being able to print whenever needed, effectively allowing you to store your print queue before release at the multifunctional printer.

    The Brother MFC-L9570CDW is a phenomenal option for any business looking for an MFP that can handle work while providing a great deal of flexibility and customizability.
