    Introducing the Canon imageFORCE C7165

    Canon has recently released a new high-performance hybrid model that can accomplish the needs of a business seeking a reliable office solution capable of handling high-speed color printing that can also support light production applications without sacrificing space or quality.

    In this article we will go over some key features and why your business may want to consider investing in this hybrid model:


    High-definition image quality with industry leading print resolutions up to 4800 X 2400 dpi.


    This multifunctional printer comes with newly developed tech called D2 exposure, this technology provides a method of light exposure during the printing process. It uses OLED as the light source and combines it with Silicon Wafer technology to help elevate print quality beyond what a traditional laser printer will do by enhancing color accuracy and resolution.

    Additionally, this model delivers vivid visual enhancement options to further ensure great clarity with every print. From the print driver, a user can select from over 10 preset profiles, this includes color enhancement options as well.

    Support for a wide variety of media and flexibility


    This model supports long sheets up to 51.2 inches

    It can come with an optional Long Sheet Feeder attachment

    It is the first enterprise model by Canon that can support printing on water resistant media due to its multipurpose tray. Water resistant material can be necessary for making items such as outdoor signage, maps, and even restaurant menus.

    It is capable of efficiently creating in-office posters, flyers, long banners, and pamphlets.

    It is also capable of printing sales materials such as business cards, and other personalized direct mail.

    Fast Production Output


    Thanks to the imageForce C7165 air-assisted paper feeding, the multifunctional printer adjusts to environmental changes and prevents paper adhesion. This greatly improves productivity when printing on coated paper.

    Furthermore, the MFP can hold up to 50 sheets of 128g paper to be loaded in one go allowing businesses to save time on needing to constantly replenish their machine.

    Enhanced Security


    With the utilization of AI and machine learning, this printer uses the latest tech in the industry. The panel itself will provide recommendations to the best security settings for the company, which removes the need for in-house expertise to make the best decisions for you. Additionally, it's capable of detecting real-time changes going on within the company network and can notify the user to help minimize risk. Packed with a Managed Print Provider like Summit, our team will also be able to provide the latest security settings and details for what's best on the model.

    Sustainable and Environmentally Friendly Design


    The imageForce C7165 model is designed with sustainability, it uses 30% of its resin material made with recycled plastic material. This is a 2% increase from the standard MFPs from Canon.

    Additionally, the C7165 is Energy Star Certified and offers a low Typical Electricity Consumption rating

    The imageForce C7165 is truly a new step for the MFP line up, with its hybrid build and modern finish, it aims to provide a variety of tools to meet many business challenges. For more information on this printer, the latest models, and the imageFORCE lineup as a whole, reach out to Summit Technology. We’re committed to helping your business by providing the right solutions and exceptional services every step of the way.
