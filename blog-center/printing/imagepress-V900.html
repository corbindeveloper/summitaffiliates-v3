    Remarkable Production Machine: The imagePress V900

    Are you looking for a machine that can offer impressive productivity with incredible automation, while still delivering high quality accurate detailed finishes. The imagePress V900 might be the solution, designed to handle light- to mid-volume production. This machine offers a variety of features and benefits that will increase the overall efficiency of your business workflow.

    In this article, we will explore some of the benefits this printer brings to the table.


    The video was made and presented by Canon Signapore

    High Consistent Productivity


    The V900 comes with what is known as High-Productivity Mode which will enable high throughput on long runs. The printer will maintain this throughout the whole production with speeds reaching 90 pages per minute. Even with the amount of speed and long-running prints, this machine is capable of real-time adjustments allowing for minimal downtime resulting in work production that will show no signs of halt.

    Advanced R-VCSEL Imaging Laser


    This 32-beam laser delivers 2400 x 2400 dots per inch print resolution, with detailed imagery and phenomenal definition in each high-speed print

    Translucent CV Toner


    Assists with production of consistent, vivid, and crisp imagery on a variety of media with a realistic, offset-like finish. This toner allows for the media surface properties to be preserved.

    Compact Registration Unit


    Designed for accurate sheet to sheet registration across long runs on large, heavy, and sturdy stocks in a fully compact design.

    Inline Spectrophotometers


    With a simple press of a button, these sensors will do color adjustments from engine linearization to media calibration. The Inline Spectrophotometers will automate your crucial color tasks.

    Multi-Sheet Detection


    The trays within this printer are capable of multi-sheet detection to assist in preventing issues from sheets that could cause jams or other hiccups to happen within the printer.

    Wealth of Finishing Options for your Print


    This imagePress also comes with optional attachments and accessories that can give you the desired finishing option for your production print. These options include but are not limited to

    Professional Punching and Creasing Finish: In-line hole punching and creasing that will assist in minimizing toner cracks on the spines of saddle-stitched booklets.


    Ring Binding: Utilizing the GBC eWire Pro, can automate the process of wire binding booklets and manuals.


    Paper Folding: Produce Z-fold, C-fold, accordion fold, and double-parallel fold documents.


    High-Capacity Stacking: Capable of stacking up to 6,000 sheets with uninterrupted printing equipped with unload while run capabilities.


    Booklet Trimming: Produce professional square-bound booklets. This assists with maintaining consistent high productivity with a large trim waste bin for on-the-fly waste removal.

    In summary, the imagePress V900 is a great choice for any business looking to output high consistent production. It’s a sturdy machine that will print to a variety of media surfaces, providing vivid detail and accuracy, all the while making sure the media remains preserved.
