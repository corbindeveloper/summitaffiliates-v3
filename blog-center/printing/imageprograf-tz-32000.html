    The Wide Format Hybrid Solution:
    The imagePROGRAF TZ-32000

    Is your business looking for a top of the line wide format printer capable of impressive speeds and a variety of solutions? Then the imagePROGRAF TZ-32000 may be the answer. This large format printer is designed with high production and versatility in mind. It has capabilities to output monochrome line drawings to vibrant full-color posters on various media substrates.

    In this article, we will dive into some of the benefits and features this printer will bring


    The video was made and presented by Canon USA


    Among the Fastest Speeds

    This imagePROGRAF is considered the fastest to date. Having a faster media loading speed from previous models by roughly 34 percent. It will comfortably print up to 4 D-size prints a minute.

    LUCIA TD 5-Color Pigment Ink System

    New magenta ink was developed for this line of imagePROGRAF which has allowed for exceptional red color reproduction. Additionally, the pigment ink used ensures high quality prints that are durable and resistant to fading. The ink used is also water resistant as well allowing for posters and outdoor signages to handle the harshest weather.

    Intelligent Media Handling

    This machine is fully capable of understanding and detecting what media is being fed through its system. This includes identifying the media type as well as its length and width. Once it finishes understanding what media it will be printing on, the device will make the necessary adjustments on its own to accommodate the material.

    Intuitive Built Design

    Built with ease of use in mind this wide format printer. This model comes with an ease of use dual roll design and a front top output stacker that can comfortably fit up to 100 ARCH E sheets. In addition, this imagePROGRAF comes with a 4.3-inch user interface screen, that will display how much ink and media is left within the printer as well as a variety of other settings and prompts.

    Z36 Scanner

    Incorporated within this wide format printer is a 36-inch lightweight scanner known as the Z36 that sits on the top of the device acting as a single-footprint. This scanner has the capabilities to scan monochrome documents at up to 13-inches per second and color documents at up to 6-inches per second. It can scan up to 1200 dots per inch (dpi).

    Integrated Security

    There is a lot of security integration with this imagePROGRAF printer to ensure the privacy of your documentation can be withheld as much as possible. Some of these security options include setting up users to need a PIN to access their print jobs and having the capability to disable USB ports, ethernet ports, and USB Direct Thumb Drive.

    Certified and Environmental Complient

    This large format printer is also ENERGY STAR certified and is currently registered as EPEAT Gold. This imagePROGRAF replaces the EPS packaging material with cardboard packaging and reduces power consumption.

    In summary, the imagePROGRAF TZ-32000 is an exceptional machine that can meet the needs of any business. It is one of the fastest wide format printers and has some of the best ink systems on the market. It's an easy to use printer that can recognize and understand the media it works with, making it very intuitive for new employees to learn. Combined with its security system and certifications, any business will be able to feel relief having this wide format on hand.
