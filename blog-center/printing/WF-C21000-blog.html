    We Are a Proud Authorized Epson Dealer

    Revolutionize with The WorkForce Enterprise WF-C21000

    Are you a business looking for a fast multifunctional printer capable of handling large amounts of printer workload? The Epson WF-C21000 may be the solution for your business needs. This machine has the fastest print speeds in its class and comes jam packed with a wealth of features and additional options.

    In this article, we will explore the benefits and features that come with this powerhouse machine and what makes this eco-friendly printer stand out.


    The video was made and presented by Epson Canada


    Top of the Line Speed

    This printer can print at speeds up to 100 ISO pages per minute in both black and/or colored ink. This is also in combination with a fast first page out time as well to produce your printed material with high efficiency. This makes the WF-C21000 the fastest within its class, while still maintaining great quality output.

    High Grade Ink

    To ensure high end color accuracy on your print jobs, this printer comes with DuraBrite Pro Ink. This fast-drying ink is designed to deliver sharp text and bright colors to output the highest professional quality results. While also offering great fade resistance and print longevity, your prints can not only come out of the printer professionally but continue to remain professional.

    Intuitive Touchscreen display

    This Epson machine comes with a 9” color touch display that is both intuitive and easy to navigate. The menu of the touch screen is also customizable and can be adjusted to fit your needs. Even the background of your platform can be customized to include your company logo or background colors.

    Document Storage Integration

    Contained within the WF-C21000 is a 320 GB hard drive. This allows for easy document access right from your control panel. The machine will create up to 199 shared folders which can store an astounding 3,000 documents and up to 15,000 pages in total.

    Storage Handling Capabilities

    Four standard cassette trays can be located at the bottom of this printer which will comfortably hold up to 550 sheets. However, in addition to the standard, the WF-C21000 can be expanded on with the high-capacity paper tray add-on. This addition will increase its storage capabilities to a staggering 5,350 sheets, which will help with office space consolidation and consistent workflow. The trays are also not limited to just paper, the storage capacity can hold all kinds of media types such as banners, card stock, and envelopes.

    Heat-Free Technology

    As with many Epson multifunctional printers, this machine eliminates the need for heat during the printing process, this lower energy consumption translates to a more environmentally friendly MFP.

    Energy Efficient

    With all its amazing tools, the WF-C21000 has the lowest power consumption among its class. Additionally, this allows for a very flexible multi-functional printer, as it can be placed anywhere in the office without needing to worry about any special electrical requirements.

    Flexible Option Enhancements

    The WF-C21000 comes with a variety of options to help expand its capabilities further. In addition to the high-capacity paper tray that can help storage. You can attach some finisher units as well to enhance your output presentation. The staple finisher unit for example, will allow your finished document to be stapled in a variety of positions and will open the possibility to output documents that needed to be stacked or collated together. The Booklet Finisher Unit will take it a step further and allow you to create full-on booklets using saddle stitch and saddle fold options.

    In summary, if you're looking for a superb multi-functional printer with a wealth of options and customization. The Epson WF-C21000 will be a great choice, offering top of the line printing jobs and with some of the highest speeds in the printer line-up.
