    The iX-3 Postage Meter

    Don’t let this tiny postage meter fool you, it may be small, but it has enough power and capabilities to fully change the entire workflow of a business. Often targeted at small companies, they are also a great option for any company size hoping for a low cost and compact tool.

    Let’s look at some of the key features this model offers:



    Cost-Effective


    Postage Meters are designed to make your workflow simpler and faster. They assist in cutting back on costs, your company would otherwise be spending on employees accomplishing the same results by hand. With the iX-3 being on the lower end of the Quadient line up, this makes it incredibly ideal for small businesses who have a large volume of mail, but don’t want to invest too much in a high costing machine. Furthermore, when partnering with a mailing dealership such as Summit Technology, we work with your business to offer flexible deals and opportunities to drive costs even further down. Giving the customer one of the best deals on the market for their postage meters.




    Grand Capabilities


    Often many businesses will think because it costs less, it must not do a lot. The beauty of the iX-3 is that despite its size and despite its low cost, it handles many tasks with great efficiency.


    This postage meter can quickly and easily process:

  • First-Class Letters

  • Postcards

  • Flat Envelopes

  • Packages

    • Not only can it process these tasks quickly, but these machines are compatible with mailing software powered by S.M.A.R.T software, that can offer tracking and reporting of your postage expenses. As well as give notifications of when shipments arrive and overall detailed reporting on how mail is handled. Allowing you as a business to plan, stay ahead, and come up with an efficient strategy for your mailing workflow.


    Postage meters also offer customizability, allowing you to print your own company logo or any professional look you desire. The system itself already has a library of over 100 images you could choose from, but the user can create their own within minutes. For the iX-3, it is capable of storing up to 10 images for instant printing at the touch of a button.


    Compact and Space Saving


    A big thing that sets the iX-3 apart is how small and easily it can fit almost anywhere. This makes the placement of the location for this machine very easy and convenient, allowing full flexibility in the workforce. And because of its size you won’t have to worry about this postage meter taking up a lot of space either, it will be seamlessly integrated within your office. If partnered with Summit, our experts will take care of the installation process as well, simply designate a location and they’ll work with you to get your new mail equipment installed quickly.


    It's Simple and Easy


    The iX-3 comes with a user-friendly control panel and offers easy to use prompts and shortcuts to make navigation of your machine easy. This is extremely important because it allows users to learn quickly and assists in training new employees, allowing them to adjust to the postage meter fast. Our Summit experts can also assist in demonstrating how the product works and give advice in how to get the best use of your new product.


    With a machine full of abundant features and precision performance packed in a small, sizable device, the iX-3 is ready to tackle any mailing challenge. Expect this postage meter to completely change the landscape of your mailing environment. For more information consider Scheduling an Assessment with Summit, Free of Charge! Our experts will do a thorough analysis and see what works right for your office and mailing requirements
