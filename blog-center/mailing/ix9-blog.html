    Mail Productivity Maximized: The iX-9 Postage Meter

    Are you a business looking for a high end machine capable of taking full control of your mailing environment? The powerful iX-9 postage meter will thoroughly optimize your mail workflow productivity and efficiency. It has been designed and crafted to automatically seal, weigh, measure, and stack large mail runs in minutes. With a 96% customer satisfaction rate, this is one of the best postage meters on the market.

    In this article, we will look at some features and benefits of the iX-9 postage meter and why your business may want to heavily consider investing in one of your own.


    The video was made and presented by Quadient


    MACHINE SPECIFICATIONS

    Processing Speed

    The iX-9 has three processing speeds to choose from, that can effortlessly batch process speeds of up to 210, 260, or 300 letters per minute. Regarded as one of the top contenders for the fastest postage meters on the market.

    Dynamic Scale

    This system accurately weighs, rates, and classifies mail at speeds of up to 160 letters per minute or 180 letters per minute for postcards.

    Configurable Design offers Return on Investment

    iX-9's are designed to be configurable so that you only purchase the necessary components and applications needed. Eliminating the need for overspending. What's more, these machines include print heads that are designed for lasting performance, matching the lifespan of the mailing machine. This feature eliminates the need for print head replacements and still provides exceptionally high-quality prints for your mail.

    Mixed Mail Feeder

    The system eliminates the need for hand sorting by size or weight. Easily handle a mix of nested or non-nested envelopes and postcards.

    Weighing Platforms

    With 10, 30 or 70lb. weighting platforms, you can easily manage even the heaviest parcels and oversized packages. The iX-9 is designed for durability, what’s more, adding differential weighing helps cut down on manual processing and handling, making your operations smoother.

    USPS Based IMI Meter

    This postage meter is optimized with the latest USPS requirements for data and security. The machine aims to guarantee compliance with Intelligent Mail Indicia.

    Intuitive Control Panel

    A color touchscreen, intuitive prompts, and handy shortcut keys ensure that navigating the system and processing mail is incredibly simple.

    Power Conveyor Stacker

    Get the most out of your operations with a high-capacity stacker that reduces downtime from constant starts and stops, ensuring maximum efficiency.

    Sealing System

    Achieve an accurate, consistent envelope seal every time with the dual-pump, jet-spray sealing system, complete with a filter. Each system offers standard spray adjustments for optimal performance.

    Optional Full Keyboard Support

    An optional integrated keyboard offers convenience while simplifying setup and mail processing further.

    ROBUST MAILING SOFTWARE INTEGRATIONS

    Acquire the Best Shipping Service with Neoship

    The iX-9 can work with shipping software like Neoship to offer you the best shipping service. This robust software will allow you to ship packages right from your desktop, all while being able to identify the lowest cost and fastest delivery for your packages. Additionally, you will be able to manage shipment expectations, with end to end online tracking, and email alerts that will let the customers know the status of their shipments.

    Enhanced further with S.M.A.R.T Integration

    With how powerful the iX-9 already is, it also integrates the best with Quadients mail center software. This greatly elevates your machine by providing the absolute best shipping, mailing, accounting, reporting, and tracking experience. All these benefits are accessed within a single user-friendly dashboard. If interested in learning more about this in greater detail, check out S.M.A.R.T – The mail solution that changed business blog.

    CONCLUSION

    These high end machines come equipped with flexible deals and leasing options as well.
