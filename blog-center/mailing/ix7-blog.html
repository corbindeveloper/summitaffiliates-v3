    The Mail System Powerhouse: The iX-7

    Are you a business with high volumes of mail? Looking to invest in a powerhouse machine that can handle your mail processing quickly and produce high quality output. The iX-7 mail machine may be the solution. From its intuitive design to its amazing durability, this postage meter is prepared to tackle even the toughest business challenges.

    The iX-7 mail machines are backed with proven results showing an astounding 96% customer satisfaction rate, making them a popular choice for growing or large-scale businesses.

    In this article, we will explore some features and benefits to the iX-7 Mail Machines


    The video was made and presented by Quadient


    Mixed Mail Feeder

    Can process up to 140 lines per minute with the standard iX-7, and can process 175 lines per minute with the iX-7 PRO.

    USPS Based IMI Meter

    This postage meter is optimized with the latest USPS requirements for data and security. The machine aims to guarantee compliance with Intelligent Mail Indicia.

    Weighing Platform

    From 10 to 30 or even 70 lb platforms are capable of handling extremely heavy parcels or overweight packages with relative ease.

    Intuitive Control Panel

    It comes with a large color touchscreen with shortcut keys to make mail processing simply and easy. The screen also offers short and easy prompts so new users can navigate the system well.

    Catch Tray

    Optimized to hold a variety of various items, this expandable tray can handle postcards to 10” x 13” envelopes

    Dynamic Scale

    The scale within the iX-7 mail machines can process up to 75 lines per minute while the iX-7Pro can reach up to 110 lines per minute.

    Envelope Sealer

    This postage meter comes jam packed with an automatic sealer that is easy to access and ensures nice clean accurate envelope seals.

    Processing of Flats and Large Media

    In comparison to smaller postage meters, the iX-7 is capable of processing large flats such as huge envelopes or mail pieces that are considerably larger than the average mail size.

    Roll Label/Tape Extension

    This postage meter can connect with roll label/tape printers, that will print labels with much better ease. No longer do you have to worry about the hassle of peel and sticking the label to mail. The printer will process the label fully ready to stick on the mailing material.

    These machines come equipped with flexible deals and leasing options as well.
