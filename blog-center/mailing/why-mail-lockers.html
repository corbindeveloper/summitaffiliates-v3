    Why Invest in Mail/Parcel Lockers?

    With package/mail theft showing no signs of slowing down and is still a major concern for many companies today. The demand for secure and efficient parcel delivery solutions has skyrocketed. We will look at some of the key benefits for Mail/Parcel Lockers and how they have significantly helped in keeping your solutions safe and secure while providing ease of flexibility for business workflow.

    Enhanced Security


    One huge reason businesses are investing in parcel lockers is due to their unparallel security. Compared to traditional storage methods, these lockers provide a reliable solution to safeguard valuable packages. Each locker is equipped with high security locks that provide strong physical protection against unauthorized access. Additionally, there are numerous layers of security added from their design, monitoring, and safety nets.


    We will explore some of these features below:


  • Advanced Access Control:

    • Parcel lockers come equipped with strong security access, such as biometric authentication or PIN code requirements. Our locker system specifically come with top of the line face recognition to confirm locker access. This ensures only authorized individuals will gain access to the lockers.


  • Tamper-Proof Design:

    • Mail lockers are made with sturdy material and tamper resistant features which hinders theft and vandalism from occurring. Additionally, these lockers are designed to be weather resistant. They can withstand extreme heat and cold, to keep the contents safe and sound. Featuring high-quality seals engineered for exceptional durability and resistance to wear and tear over time.


  • Real-time Tracking:

    • Many mail locker systems offer tracking and notifications, which allow recipients to monitor the status of their deliveries and plan accordingly. Users can access their lockers through a mobile app to see these notifications and even unlock their assigned compartments remotely. They provide a user-friendly interface which allows for ease of use and navigation. Additionally, should something unexpected happen with your package, you would be alerted immediately.


    Flexibility and Convenience


    Parcel lockers, in addition to offering robust security, bring a lot of ease and convenience to package handling. These lockers are constructed in a way to meet with your business needs, so there is no disruption in business workflow. Many customers praise these lockers not only in the United States but worldwide as well. In fact, countries such as France and Japan showed an astounding 97% customer satisfaction with both their pickup and drop-off experience using these lockers. Here are a few reasons why:



  • Full Time Accessibility:

    • Customers have 24/7 access to these lockers, so they can pick up their package at their own pace or time. Additionally, because alerts can be sent out of your package arriving, this allows time to work around any business complications. Your mail will remain in a safe place till the time comes for you to pick it up.


  • Scalability:

    • Mail locker systems can be scaled easily to accommodate varying delivery volumes, thus they become suitable for businesses of all varying sizes. Businesses can also customize locker configurations to meet their specific needs, especially useful for the various packages that would get delivered. They also come with cloud based management platforms that work alongside the growing number of lockers, users, and/or packages that get delivered.


  • Maintenance and Support:

    • Additionally, Parcel Lockers come with frequent maintenance to ensure good up to date equipment. There are designated technicians that can repair these lockers and if through a managed mail provider expect ease of contact to address any issues regarding them.


    Parcel Lockers are a secure ease of use tool that can help support your business growth.
