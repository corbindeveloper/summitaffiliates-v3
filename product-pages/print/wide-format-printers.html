    Frequently Asked Questions

    Explore the expansive capabilities and superior quality of our wide format printers. Find answers to common questions about their large scale printing functions, and how they can elevate your business's production capabilities.

    What media types can your wide format printers handle?
    Our wide format printers are versatile, supporting a variety of media including glossy, satin, matte papers, vinyl, canvas, and more.
    How large can your printers produce images?
    Our models can print images ranging from 17 inches up to 98 inches wide with varying lengths, catering to a vast array of large format printing needs.
    Do your wide format printers support borderless printing?
    Yes, our printers offer borderless printing capabilities, ideal for creating seamless large scale graphics and banners.
    What resolution can I expect from your wide format printers?
    Our wide format printers offer high resolution printing with some models going up to 2400 x 1200 dpi, ensuring crisp and vivid prints.
