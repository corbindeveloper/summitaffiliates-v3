    Frequently Asked Questions

    At Summit Technology Affiliates, our commitment stretches across a robust portfolio of companies, each dedicated to excellence. Known for merging precision in print, efficiency in mailing, and the dynamic realm of digital communications.

    What types of documents can your scanners process?

    Our scanners are versatile and can process a wide range of documents, including standard paper documents, business cards, ID cards, photos, and even fragile or oversized documents. They are designed to handle various sizes and types of media with high precision and care.

    How do your scanners ensure the quality of scans?

    Our scanners are equipped with advanced imaging technologies that ensure high resolution scans with excellent clarity and color accuracy. They feature automatic image enhancement functions to improve readability, remove imperfections, and ensure that each scan is a true and accurate digital representation of the original document.

    Can these scanners integrate with existing document management systems?

    Yes, our scanners are designed for easy integration with most existing document management systems. They support various digital formats and can connect to networks or cloud services, ensuring seamless integration into your workflow and facilitating immediate access to scanned documents.

    What support and maintenance services are available for your scanners?

    We offer comprehensive support and maintenance services for all our scanners. This includes technical support, warranty services, and regular maintenance checks to ensure optimal performance. Our team is also available for on-site repairs and troubleshooting to address any issues promptly.
