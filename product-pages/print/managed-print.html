Frequently Asked Questions

At Summit Technology Affiliates, our commitment stretches across a robust portfolio of companies, each dedicated to excellence. Known for merging precision in print, efficiency in mailing, and the dynamic realm of digital communications.

What are the key benefits of Managed Print Services?

Managed Print Services (MPS) offer a range of benefits including significant cost reductions, improved efficiency, reduced environmental impact, and enhanced security. By optimizing your print infrastructure and streamlining management processes, MPS help in minimizing waste and promoting sustainable practices.

How does MPS help in reducing printing costs?

MPS reduces printing costs by optimizing printer fleets, reducing unnecessary printing, improving supply management, and ensuring efficient maintenance. This leads to lower ink and paper usage, reduced downtime, and longer equipment lifespans, all contributing to cost savings.

Can Managed Print Services improve document security?

Yes, MPS enhance document security through secure printing practices, user authentication, and advanced monitoring tools. By controlling access to printers and tracking usage, MPS help in safeguarding sensitive information and ensuring compliance with data protection regulations.

What kind of businesses can benefit from MPS?

Businesses of all sizes and across various sectors can benefit from MPS. Whether it's a small business seeking to streamline its printing processes or a large corporation aiming to optimize a complex print environment, MPS offer scalable and customizable solutions to meet diverse needs.
